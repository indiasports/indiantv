eSewa Nepal


Esewa Nepal is both online (via website) and offline (via mobile app and SMS Syntax) wallet made by F1 Soft company with the collaboration with phonepay. This wallet has many payment and receipt option. We can receive fund from Banks and Western Union similarly make payment of phone Bill, internet bill, Dish home, SimTV, Credit card payments, Electricity bill, Drinking water Bill, mobile top up, get recharge cards ( mobile phones, dish TV) and many more.
Esewa has easy registration facility via phone number but we can get verified sending Scan PP size photo and citizenship copy. The company has reward facility which can be redeemed with certain terms and conditions.
eSewa is Nepal's first payment gateway with integrated digital wallet. You can register on-the-go and save Rs. 500 - Rs. 2000 in a month.
How to register and make payment with eSewa?
Download eSewa app and click on Register. Your mobile number will be your eSewa ID.
Load fund to eSewa
You can load fund to your eSewa account in 4 different ways:
1. Via Mobile/Internet Banking:
You can load eSewa wallet from your internet/mobile banking without any additional cost from 47 banks. Visit your bank branch if you have not enabled internet banking and mobile banking.
