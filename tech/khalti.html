What is Khalti?


Khalti is a revolutionary way to make payments in Nepal. It offers the safest and fastest online payment experience for various Bill Payments, Mobile Recharges, DTH payments, Money Transfers, Domestic Remittance, shopping and wallet payments. It is an easy way to send money or accept payments instantly. Khalti is one stop payment gateway that provides range of services including online mobile recharge, bill payments, online ticketing and many more. Khalti is operated and owned by Sparrow Pay Pvt. Ltd. Our parent company Janaki Technology has been in the software product industry since 2010.
Khalti also allows money transfers & payments via various bank accounts & Agent Network. It also provides various offers & cashbacks on various service payments. A lot of new exciting services & offers will be added soon.

How to load Khalti?

You can load your wallet using internet banking, mobile banking, debit/credit card, bank deposit, kiosk machines, Khalti Pasals or simply requesting your friend to load your Khalti via their account. Currently over 40 banks are available to load funds in Khalti, with more banks coming soon!
